The Studio

Built to
Build Things

AscendForge is an independent game studio. We build on-chain games for web3.

The name means what it says. A forge is where raw material gets hammered into something that holds. That's how we approach every title: deliberate craft, no shortcuts, shipped when it's right.

We're small and intend to stay that way. Quality over catalogue.

Follow on X

On-chain where it matters. Not everything needs to be on-chain. But when it is, it's real. Proper wallet flows, real ownership.

Ship and iterate. We'd rather have a working game in your hands than a perfect game on a whiteboard.

Quality over catalogue. Fewer titles, done properly.
